Short Biography

With over 27 years of experience as an education officer in Secondary Schools and Junior Colleges, I have acquired a diverse range of teaching experiences. These experiences have equipped me with the ability to know the SEC/JC Chinese curriculum in depth and in breadth. I was awarded the Inspiring Chinese Language Teachers Award by the Ministry of Education in 2011 for my passion and dedication to teaching students. My robust foundation in the teaching of the Chinese Language gives me the ability to lead my students to academic excellence.
